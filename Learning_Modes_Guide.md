# AR英语学习应用 - 三种学习模式指南

## ? 三种学习模式简介

### ? Normal Mode (普通聊天模式)
**特点**：自由对话 + 自动语法纠错
- 与AI自然聊天，讨论任何话题
- AI回复后自动提供语法纠错和改进建议
- 适合日常英语练习和提升流利度

### ?? Scene Mode (场景学习模式)  
**特点**：基于真实场景的互动学习
- 展示图片或周围环境给AI
- AI分析场景并开始相关话题对话
- 学习场景相关的词汇和表达方式

### ? Word Mode (单词学习模式)
**特点**：专注词汇学习和练习
- 展示物品图片给AI识别
- AI教授1-2个核心词汇
- 练习单词用法、造句和对话

---

## ? 模式切换方法

### 方法1：点击按钮
- **Normal Mode按钮**：切换到普通聊天
- **Scene Mode按钮**：切换到场景学习  
- **Word Mode按钮**：切换到单词学习
- 当前模式的按钮会显示为?**绿色**

### 方法2：文本命令
直接在聊天框输入：
- `"normal mode"` 或 `"chat mode"` → 普通模式
- `"scene mode"` 或 `"scene learning"` → 场景模式
- `"word mode"` 或 `"vocabulary"` → 单词模式

## ? Agent Button 演示对话样例

> **Agent Button说明**：语音输入按钮，会根据当前模式和用户语音内容智能判断是否需要获取场景信息

### ? Normal Mode - Agent Button 演示

**操作**：按住Agent Button说话

**用户语音**：*"Hello, how are you today?"*

**AI回复**：Hello! I'm doing great, thank you for asking! How has your day been so far? Are you ready to practice some English together?

**用户语音**：*"I go to school yesterday and meet my friends."*

**AI回复**：That sounds wonderful! Meeting friends at school is always nice. Did you have classes together or just hang out?

**语法纠错**：
> ? **Grammar Tip**: 
> - Original: "I go to school yesterday and meet my friends"
> - Corrected: "I went to school yesterday and met my friends"
> - Explanation: Use past tense "went" and "met" when talking about yesterday
> - Better version: "I went to school yesterday and met up with my friends"

---

### ?? Scene Mode - Agent Button 演示

**操作**：按住Agent Button说话

**用户语音**：*"Tell me about this place"* 或 *"What do you see here?"*

**AI分析场景**：? *[自动获取周围环境]*

**AI回复**：I can see you're in what looks like a cozy café! There are tables and chairs around, and the lighting creates a warm atmosphere. This seems like a perfect place to relax. Do you come here often?

**用户语音**：*"Yes, I like drink coffee here"*

**AI回复**：That's great! Coffee shops are wonderful places to unwind. What's your favorite type of coffee? Do you prefer something strong like espresso, or maybe something sweeter like a latte?

**用户语音**：*"I like cappuccino with sugar"*

**AI回复**：Cappuccino with sugar is a classic choice! The foam on top makes it extra special. Have you ever tried making cappuccino at home, or do you prefer getting it from professional baristas like here?

---

### ? Word Mode - Agent Button 演示

**操作**：按住Agent Button说话

**用户语音**：*"What is this?"* 或 *"Teach me about this object"*

**AI分析物品**：? *[自动获取物品图像]*

**AI回复**：I see a **smartphone**! Let me teach you about this word.

**Smartphone** means a mobile phone that can connect to the internet and run applications.

**Example sentences**:
- "I use my smartphone to call my friends."
- "This smartphone has a very good camera."

**Mini dialogue**:
- A: "Can I borrow your smartphone for a moment?"
- B: "Sure, but the battery is almost dead."

Now try making your own sentence with "smartphone"!

**用户语音**：*"I buy new smartphone last week"*

**AI回复**：Great attempt! The correct sentence is: "I **bought a** new smartphone last week." Remember to use past tense "bought" and add "a" before "new smartphone."

**Practice more**: Can you tell me what you use your smartphone for most often?

**用户语音**：*"I use it for chat with friends and play games"*

**AI回复**：Excellent! Almost perfect! We can say: "I use it **to chat** with friends and **to play** games." We use "to" before verbs after "use it for."

Now you've learned both "smartphone" and how to talk about its uses!

## ?? Agent Button 使用技巧

### Normal Mode 语音提示：
- 自然对话：*"How's the weather today?"*
- 描述经历：*"I had a great weekend"*
- 提问学习：*"Can you help me with English?"*

### Scene Mode 语音提示：
- 场景询问：*"What do you think about this place?"*
- 环境描述：*"Tell me about what you see"*
- 比较讨论：*"Is this similar to places in your country?"*

### Word Mode 语音提示：
- 物品识别：*"What is this called in English?"*
- 词汇学习：*"Teach me about this object"*
- 用法练习：*"How do I use this word in a sentence?"*

### ? 模式切换语音命令：
- *"Switch to normal mode"* → 切换到普通模式
- *"Change to scene mode"* → 切换到场景模式  
- *"Go to word mode"* → 切换到单词模式

## ? 快速开始建议

### 新用户推荐流程：
1. **先试试Normal Mode** - 随便聊天，熟悉AI的对话风格
2. **体验Scene Mode** - 拍张周围环境照片，看AI如何分析
3. **练习Word Mode** - 拍个物品照片，学习具体词汇

### 高效学习技巧：
- **Normal Mode**：不要怕犯语法错误，AI会自动纠正
- **Scene Mode**：多描述场景细节，AI会提供更丰富的对话
- **Word Mode**：主动造句练习，加深词汇记忆

每种模式都有独特的学习价值，建议根据当前需求灵活切换使用！
